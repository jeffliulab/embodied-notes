# Industry Updates

## Overview

2024-2025 is a critical turning point for embodied intelligence, **transitioning from concept to product**. Large model technology is permeating the robotics domain, capital is flowing in abundantly, multiple companies have released or updated humanoid robot products, and policy support is strengthening globally. This article tracks major events chronologically.

## VI. 2025 Outlook

### 6.1 Technology

- **VLA model scaling**: From 1B parameters to 10B+, cross-task generalization significantly improves
- **World model applications**: Cosmos-type models begin producing useful synthetic data
- **Dexterous hand breakthroughs**: Multiple companies release commercially viable dexterous hand products

### 6.2 Products

- **Tesla Optimus first external customers** (expected late 2025)
- **Figure 02 normalized operations at BMW plant**
- **Unitree G1 education/research market explosion**

### 6.3 Market

- **Humanoid robots from POC to small-batch production**
- **Collaborative robot market maintaining 30%+ growth**
- **First shakeout of embodied intelligence startups**

!!! warning "Uncertainty"
    The embodied intelligence field is in a period of rapid change. The above outlook is based on information available through 2025 Q1. Actual developments may deviate significantly due to technology breakthroughs or market changes.
