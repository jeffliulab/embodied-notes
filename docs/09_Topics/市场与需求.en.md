# Market and Demand

## Overview

The embodied intelligence market is expanding from traditional industrial robots to broader scenarios. This article analyzes demand drivers, typical application scenarios, market size estimates, and representative companies by industry segment.

---

## I. Manufacturing

### 1.1 Application Scenarios

| Scenario | Specific Tasks | Automation Difficulty | Current Solution |
|----------|---------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **Assembly** | Part insertion, screw tightening, harness routing | High | Manual + simple jigs |
| **Quality Inspection** | Appearance defects, dimensional inspection, weld checking | Medium | Manual + AOI |
| **Material Handling** | Loading/unloading, inter-station transport, palletizing | Low-Medium | AGV + manual |
| **Welding/Painting** | Arc welding, spot welding, spraying | Low | Traditional industrial robots |
| **Flexible Manufacturing** | High-mix low-volume line changeover | Very high | Mainly manual |

### 1.2 Market Size

- **Traditional industrial robots**: ~$160B (2025 global)
- **Collaborative robots**: ~$12B (2025), 30%+ annual growth
- **Humanoid factory robots**: ~$0.5B (2025), expected $15-30B by 2030

---

## II. Logistics and Warehousing

### 2.1 Application Scenarios

| Scenario | Specific Tasks | Challenge | Current Solution |
|----------|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Picking** | Retrieve items from shelves/bins | Item diversity, occlusion | Mainly manual |
| **Packing** | Boxing, sealing | Irregular items | Semi-automated |
| **Transport** | Pallet/tote transfer | Navigation, avoidance | AMR + manual |
| **Last-mile delivery** | Final-mile delivery | Outdoor unstructured environment | Manual |

### 2.2 Demand Drivers

- **E-commerce explosion**: Global e-commerce orders growing 15%+ annually
- **SKU explosion**: Per-warehouse SKU counts growing from thousands to tens of thousands
- **Speed competition**: Same-day/next-day delivery demands higher warehouse efficiency
- **Labor costs**: Warehouse worker annual turnover 60%+ (US)

### 2.3 Market Size

- **Warehouse robots (AMR/AGV)**: ~$8B (2025)
- **Picking robots**: ~$3B (2025), 25% annual growth
- **Last-mile delivery robots**: ~$0.5B (2025), still early stage

---

## III. Healthcare

### 3.1 Application Scenarios

| Scenario | Specific Tasks | Key Requirements | Maturity |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Surgical robots** | Minimally invasive surgery assistance | Sub-mm precision, force feedback | High (commercialized) |
| **Rehabilitation robots** | Limb movement rehabilitation training | Safety, adaptiveness | Medium |
| **Nursing assistance** | Turning, lifting, feeding | Compliant control, safety | Low |
| **Disinfection** | Hospital environment disinfection | Coverage, autonomous navigation | Medium |

### 3.2 Market Size

- **Surgical robots**: ~$8B (2025), da Vinci dominant
- **Rehabilitation robots**: ~$3B (2025)
- **Nursing robots**: ~$0.5B (2025), highest growth potential

## IV. Agriculture

### 4.1 Application Scenarios

| Scenario | Specific Tasks | Challenge | Maturity |
|----------|---------------|-----------|----------|
| **Harvesting** | Fruit/vegetable picking | Ripeness judgment, gentle grasping | Medium |
| **Weeding** | Precision weeding (reduce herbicides) | Crop/weed identification | Medium-High |
| **Spraying** | Precision pesticide/fertilizer application | Coverage, dosage control | High |
| **Inspection** | Crop growth monitoring, pest detection | Large area coverage | High (drones) |

### 4.2 Market Size

- **Agricultural drones**: ~$5B (2025)
- **Agricultural ground robots**: ~$3B (2025)
- **Harvesting robots**: ~$0.5B (2025), 35% annual growth

---

## V. Construction

### 5.1 Market Size and Drivers

- Market size: ~$1.5B (2025), expected $5B+ by 2030
- Core drivers: Construction worker aging (average age 42 and rising), high accident rates (construction injury rate 2-3x manufacturing)

## VII. Market Landscape Summary

|----------|----------|-----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Manufacturing | $170B+ | $250B+ | Labor shortage, flexible production | High |
| Logistics | $12B | $30B+ | E-commerce growth | Medium-High |

| Construction | $1.5B | $5B+ | Worker shortage, safety | Low-Medium |

    The above market sizes are estimates based on multiple research institution reports, for reference only. The embodied intelligence field is changing extremely rapidly, and actual development may significantly deviate from forecasts.
