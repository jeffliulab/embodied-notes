# Embodied Intelligence Industry Research

## Overview

The embodied intelligence industry is at a critical turning point from "technology validation" to "commercial deployment." Multiple authoritative institutions have published market forecast reports, and capital markets are pouring into this space with unprecedented enthusiasm. This article systematically surveys market size forecasts, investment dynamics, supply chain structure, and competitive landscape.

---

## Market Size Forecasts

### Major Institution Predictions

| Institution | Forecast Scope | Market Size | Timeline | Key Assumptions |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Goldman Sachs** | Global humanoid robot market | $38B | 2035 | Manufacturing and home service dual drivers |
| **Goldman Sachs (Optimistic)** | Global humanoid robot market | $154B | 2035 | Technology breakthroughs + rapid cost decline |
| **McKinsey** | Global humanoid robot market | $6-12B | 2030 | Industrial scenarios first to scale |
| **Citi** | Global humanoid robot market | $7B | 2030 | Conservative estimate, B2B focus |
| **China MIIT** | China robot industry | 300B RMB | 2025 (14th Five-Year Plan) | Includes industrial + service + special robots |

### Goldman Sachs Report Key Points

Goldman Sachs' 2024 humanoid robot deep-dive report is the industry's most influential research:

**Base scenario ($38B/2035):**
- Manufacturing deployment: ~1M humanoid robots in factories
- Service deployment: ~500K units in logistics, retail, etc.
- Unit cost: $30,000-50,000
- Annual production: ~1.5M units/year by 2035

**Optimistic scenario ($154B/2035):**
- Technology breakthroughs bring manipulation capability close to human level
- Unit cost drops below $20,000
- Home service market opens
- Annual production exceeds 5M units

## Supply Chain Structure

### Full Chain Analysis

#### Upstream: Core Components

| Component | Tech Points | Major Suppliers | Domestic Rate |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Servo motors** | High power density, high response | Maxon, TQ, Leadshine | Medium |
| **Reducers** | Harmonic/planetary/RV | Harmonic Drive, Leadshine, ZDLD | Improving |
| **Encoders** | High-precision absolute | Heidenhain, Tamagawa, Inovance | Lower |
| **Force/torque sensors** | 6-axis force sensing | ATI, OnRobot, Kunwei | Lower |
| **Computing platforms** | Edge AI inference | NVIDIA Jetson, Horizon | Improving |

#### Downstream: Application Scenarios

- **Manufacturing**: Auto assembly, electronics assembly, material handling
- **Logistics**: Sorting, handling, palletizing
- **Inspection**: Power inspection, pipeline inspection, campus security
- **Healthcare**: Rehabilitation, nursing assistance
- **Home service**: Long-term vision, not yet mature
